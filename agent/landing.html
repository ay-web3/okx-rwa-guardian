the problem

Smart contracts can't see the physical world

When a major earthquake destroys a tokenized building, the blockchain doesn't know. Lending protocols keep issuing loans. Insurance doesn't trigger. Nobody knows until it's too late.

✕ Without RWA Guardian

A 7.5 magnitude earthquake hits Noto Peninsula. The tokenized estate is destroyed. The on-chain token keeps trading at full price. Lending protocols issue loans against rubble. The market corrects days later — after millions in losses.

✓ With RWA Guardian

Our AI swarm detects the earthquake from USGS, cross-references local news and economic data, and returns a signed decision: raiseCollateralRatio. The lending protocol tightens requirements automatically — within seconds.
